Vision

Brigham Young University is an accredited institutional member of the National Association of Schools of Dance. The BYU Department of Dance provides a broad base for professional training in both the classroom and the studio. Our versatile undergraduate program includes four degrees: a Bachelor of Arts (BA) in Dance, a Bachelor of Arts (BA) in Dance Education K-12, a Bachelor of Fine Arts (BFA) in Dance and a Bachelor of Fine Arts (BFA) in Music Dance Theatre.
