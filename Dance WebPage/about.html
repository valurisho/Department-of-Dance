Meet the team

Ariana Brooks
Department Chair, Professor

Ariana Brooks brings a wealth of experience and a deep passion for dance to the Department of Dance at our university. As an Assistant Professor, Ariana is dedicated to nurturing the next generation of dancers and fostering an environment of creativity, expression, and excellence.

Ethan Martinez
Associate Professor, Department of Dance

Trained in a variety of dance styles including modern, jazz, and tap, Ethan's journey as a dancer has been marked by both passion and perseverance. His innate talent and relentless pursuit of excellence have earned him recognition on national stages.

Alexander Johnson
Administrative Assistant

Alexander is committed to creating an inclusive learning environment where students are empowered to explore their creativity, and discover their own unique artistic voices. His engaging teaching style, coupled with his genuine enthusiasm for dance, fosters a sense of mutual respect among students.
